Meet Stacey White

First Step Cleaning began with a vision that God placed on my heart—one that has grown into a purpose far greater than I ever imagined.

As a single mother, a woman whose life has been transformed by God's grace, and someone who believes every challenge has a purpose, I've learned that the smallest step can change everything. That belief is the foundation of this company.

To me, cleaning has never been just about making a space look good. It's about creating peace in the middle of life's chaos. A clean, well-cared-for home or property brings clarity, comfort, and manageability—and that is something I never take for granted.

As the Visionary Founder of First Step Cleaning, I'm personally invested in every relationship we build and every standard we set. We don't just clean spaces—we serve people with integrity, excellence, and genuine care.

First Step Cleaning stands as a reminder that purpose often grows from the most unexpected places.

But God.
